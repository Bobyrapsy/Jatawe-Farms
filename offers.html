    Agric Real-Estate

    Jawate Farms Is'nt a stagnant agricultural Organisation. As we grow, we tend to learn how to adapt faster to the changes around us. Now, this not only applies to the climate change, or sudden change in stock flow. Our ability to adapt has grown to the point where, we can now apply other aspects of leading buisnesses in the world, into Agriculture. One way we've been able to do so is through our Agricultural Real-Estate system. Now it might seem funny, probably complicated, but as a matter of fact it is possible. From our vast expanses of lands, Plots can be assigned to interested investors who wish to invest. The simple system goes as follows: 1. Rent a plot or more portions of land 2. The rent fee will include cost of total crop maintenance for the minimum required or prefered duration of the rent 3. Yeild all profits of crops sold from your rented land. There will be no charging from any profits made. All earnings shall belong to the investor as our compensation for labour and materials for crop rearing will also be included in the rent fee.
    It's that simple. For more details, contacts us, we are always at your service.
